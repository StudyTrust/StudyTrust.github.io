Roles & Responsibilities
BT
Dashboards - Board-of-Trustees
Approves Tier 1 & High Strategic policies (quorum ≥50%+1). Fiduciary oversight and King V compliance. Receives all Tier 2 & 3 notifications.
ND
National Director
Approves Tier 2 & High Operational policies. Chairs approval meetings. Cannot delegate Board-level approvals. Commences emergency reviews within 7 or 30 days.
DN
Deputy National Director
Acts in ND's absence when formally delegated in writing with defined scope and duration. Board notified if delegation exceeds 90 days.
RL
Regulatory Watch Lead
Oversees all policy development. Coordinates reviews and version control. Member of PTT. Notifies ND & Chairperson of regulatory changes.
PL
PTT Lead
Custodian of Policy Register. Manages SharePoint & HR Manage. Assigns version numbers and watermarks. Primary contact for all policy inquiries.
PO
Policy Owner
Drafts and updates policy content using the official template. Ensures legal compliance. Oversees training and enforcement. Initiates review cycles.
Administration — Group Configuration
Configure the Azure AD group display names used for role detection. These must match exactly the group names in your Azure AD tenant.

Teams Webhook Notifications
Configure Teams incoming webhook URLs for each audience. When a policy or SOP status changes or a new document is registered, an Adaptive Card notification is posted automatically to the relevant Teams channels. To create a webhook: open a Teams channel → click ••• → Connectors (or Workflows) → Incoming Webhook → create and copy the URL.
Webhook URLs are stored in your browser (localStorage). Each person who configures webhooks on their device will trigger notifications from that device. For centralised notifications, configure webhooks on one designated admin device, or use Power Automate to connect the SharePoint lists directly to Teams — see the note below.
Power Automate (recommended for reliable centralised delivery): Create a flow in Power Automate that triggers when a SharePoint list item is modified. The flow posts an Adaptive Card to your Teams channel. This works even when no one has the dashboard open, and does not depend on any single person's browser. Your IT person can set this up in about 30 minutes using the built-in SharePoint → Teams connector.
